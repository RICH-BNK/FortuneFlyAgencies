What is a Certificate of Sponsorship?

A Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) is an official document issued by a UK employer that proves they are sponsoring you for a Skilled Worker visa. It's your entry ticket to working in the UK.

Who needs it?

Anyone applying for a UK Skilled Worker visa, Senior or Specialist Worker visa, or other sponsored work routes. The CoS is issued by your employer and submitted with your visa application.

How we help you get it

We connect you with employers, guide your application, ensure you meet all requirements, and prepare you for the interview. We don't stop until you have your CoS in hand.
