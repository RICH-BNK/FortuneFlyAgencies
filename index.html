UK Student Visa

🇬🇧 UK
For international students accepted into a UK university or college. Includes sponsored study and limited work rights.
Processing: 3-8 weeks Fee: ~£490

UK Skilled Worker Visa

🇬🇧 UK
For professionals with a job offer from a UK employer. Requires sponsorship and a minimum salary threshold.
Processing: 3-8 weeks Fee: ~£719

UK Visitor Visa

🇬🇧 UK
For tourism, family visits, or short business trips. Valid for up to 6 months.

US F-1 Student Visa

🇺🇸 USA
For academic students enrolled in a US university. Allows on-campus work and OPT after graduation.
Processing: 2-6 weeks Fee: ~$160 + SEVIS

US H-1B Work Visa

📋 Required documents

Prepare these 7 items — we'll guide you through each one.

Bio page scan, valid for visa duration.

Education certificates
